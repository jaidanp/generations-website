About Generations

Generations is a revolutionary web application designed to bridge the gaps between families across the globe. Our unique algorithm cross-references individual nuclear families, helping you discover relatives you never knew you had. We believe in the power of connection and the richness of shared heritage.

Our Mission

To empower individuals to connect with their extended families, foster a deeper understanding of their lineage, and build a more interconnected global community. We strive to make family history accessible and exciting for everyone.

How It Works

Users input their nuclear family data, and our advanced algorithm analyzes commonalities and potential links across our vast database. We then present you with potential family connections, allowing you to explore and verify new relationships securely.
