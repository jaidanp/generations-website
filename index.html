Our Story

Where roots meet
the future

Generations was born from a real need — our own enormous family. When your family grows so large that it's hard to stay connected, you build something that brings everyone back together. A free, open platform where any family can enter a code, link up, and discover their extended network.

Our Mission

Empower people to connect with extended family, explore lineage, and build a more interconnected global community — completely free.

How It Works

Your family admin generates a unique family code. Members sign in, enter the code, and instantly join your family's private tree on the app.

Built with Heart

This is a family project, not a corporation. We're building in the open and keeping it free because heritage should be accessible to everyone.
